Synopsis / Description

Plot Summary

In the near future, Earth is becoming uninhabitable due to crop blights and dust storms. Former NASA pilot Cooper is tasked with piloting a spacecraft through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity. The mission takes the crew to distant planets where they encounter extreme environments and time dilation effects that challenge their mission and personal relationships.
